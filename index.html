Our Heritage

Nestled in the heart of Turin, L'Acino stands as a beacon of authentic Piedmontese cuisine. Our restaurant honors the culinary traditions passed down through generations, where every dish tells a story of the Piedmont region's rich gastronomic heritage.

Founded on the principle that true luxury lies in authenticity, we source only the finest ingredients from local producers and regional suppliers. Our kitchen is a temple of tradition, where time-honored recipes meet contemporary presentation.

At L'Acino, we believe that dining is not merely eating—it's an experience, a journey through the flavors and soul of Northern Italy.

Our Sommelier's Selection

Piedmont is one of Italy's greatest wine regions, and our extensive cellar showcases the very best. From the powerful Barolos to the delicate Moscatos, each bottle has been personally selected by our expert sommelier.

We believe wine should elevate your dining experience, not complicate it. Our sommelier is available to guide you through perfect pairings for each course.

Barolo

The "King of Wines"

Full-bodied with complex tannins, perfect with our brasato

Barbaresco

The "Queen of Wines"

Elegant and refined, pairs beautifully with pasta dishes

Moscato d'Asti

Perfect aperitif or dessert pairing
